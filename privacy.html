HKEX StockCode Checker - Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 02, 2026

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

It only operates on the official HKEX e-Submission website (esubmission.hkex.com.hk) to help users avoid uploading PDF files with incorrect stock codes.

The extension uses chrome.storage locally to temporarily remember the Company Code (last 4 digits) during one submission session. This data is stored only on your own device and is automatically cleared when the browser is closed.

No data is sent to any server, and no analytics or tracking is performed.
